Creating Efficiency Through AI

AI is not the goal — better, faster, more reliable work is. These are real examples of how I use Claude as a working tool: to remove friction, catch what humans miss, and free up time for the thinking that actually matters.

ZBB Sanity Check & Budgeting Suggestions

Instead of manually hunting for formula errors, I run a ZBB (Zero Based Budgeting) file through Claude for a full sanity check. It flags inconsistencies between assumptions, identifies gaps in cost coverage, spots formula logic issues, and suggests information I might be missing — all in seconds. What used to take hours of review is now a structured, repeatable quality gate.

Automated NetSuite → Cost Review Workflow

I built a custom Claude skill that automates the full end-to-end cost review update. When a new NetSuite file lands in a folder, Claude cleans the data (fills blank GL account names, resolves missing vendor references), replaces the source tab in the cost review workbook, and saves a new versioned file — fully hands-off. A process that previously required 20–30 minutes of careful manual work now runs in under a minute, with zero risk of human error.

Finance Ops Systems Design Operational Infrastructure

Close Operations Infrastructure — 7 Initiatives

Seven structural improvements designed to eliminate recurring close friction at the root cause — from a one-page booking rules playbook to a KPI dashboard tracking FP&A time on prep vs. analysis. Each initiative targets a specific data quality or coordination gap that compounds every month.

Close Operations Infrastructure · Additional Improvements Proposal · April 2026
01
Booking Rules Playbook
One-page checklist covering vendor naming, GL selection, departments, descriptions, and contractor tagging. Stored in Teams and linked from the Planner task. Stops data hygiene issues at the source.
02
Shared Finance & Accounting Calendar
Business-day-based calendar with owner, reviewer, and due date for every deliverable. Same file month-over-month. Eliminates email reminders and gives predictability across the team.
03
Close Kick-off & Debrief Cadence
15-min kick-off on Day −1 to align; 30-min debrief after each close to capture what went wrong and assign fixes. Turns each close into a learning cycle instead of repeating the same issues.
04
Claude Cowork Automation Roadmap
Team-wide assessment of tasks and sub-tasks to identify automation opportunities. Each Claude skill removes hours of manual prep and keeps outputs consistent month-over-month.
05
Shared SharePoint Close Folder
One folder per close with fixed subfolders: NetSuite extracts, MRR, Payroll, Reviews. Replaces email attachments with version control and traceability so anyone can pick up where someone left off.
06
Close KPIs Dashboard
Tracks days-to-close, post-close corrections, late deliverables, and FP&A hours on prep vs. analysis. Makes the impact of process improvements visible and drives ongoing accountability. Built with Claude.
07
Cross-functional Data Normalisation
Shared naming standards with GTM, R&D, and HR for vendors, staff, products, and business units. Reduces reconciliation effort across every reporting cycle and enables cleaner cross-functional analysis.
