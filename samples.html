Process Improvement & Automation

Workflow improvements built to eliminate manual steps, reduce reporting lag, and create self-serve tools for Finance and cross-functional teams.

Finance Operations Calendar

A centralised, automated calendar to manage global reporting deadlines across time zones. Ensures 100% compliance with close cycles and proactively surfaces housekeeping tasks — expiring subscriptions, vendor contract renewals, and quarterly reviews.

Dynamic Reporting with Pivot Charts & Slicers

Replaced static, tab-heavy reports with a single source of truth enhanced with Pivot Charts, slicers, and timelines — enabling self-serve navigation for non-finance stakeholders without requiring manual refreshes or analyst support.

02

Business Partnering

Finance work built to empower non-finance stakeholders — turning data into decisions and giving teams true ownership of their numbers.

FP&A Case Example — Model Conclusions

A walkthrough of a pipeline-driven forecast model, highlighting key assumptions, scenario outputs, and the strategic conclusions surfaced for executive and board-level decision-making.

GTM Recommendation

A finance-backed Go-To-Market recommendation built for a Sales and Marketing team. Translates financial modelling into a clear strategic narrative — showing ROI, payback period, and risk scenarios in a language non-finance partners can act on.

03

Excel Modelling

Financial models built for clarity, flexibility, and real decision-making. Audit-ready, easy to hand off, and built to scale as the business grows.

FP&A Case Example — Pipeline Driven Forecast Model

A dynamic forecast model driven by pipeline data. Converts sales funnel inputs into revenue projections with scenario toggles, enabling Finance and Sales leadership to stress-test assumptions and align on a single plan. Includes a dynamic Dashboard with insights ready to use in a slide presentation.
