Model assumptions
  1. Revenue recognition: Straight-line over the contract term per ASC 606 — equal amount each month regardless of billing frequency.
  2. Monthly billing — collection lag: Invoice issued same month as delivery; cash collected 30 days later, so AR = 1 month of revenue outstanding at month-end.
  3. Monthly billing — Unbilled AR: $0, because billing and delivery are in the same period. It only appears when there is a lag between delivery and invoicing.
  4. Annual / quarterly upfront — AR: $0 throughout; cash is received before or at delivery. No collection risk on future periods.
  5. RPO disclosure: Footnote under ASC 606, not a balance sheet line. For upfront billing, RPO = Deferred Revenue (same unearned amount, different lenses).
  6. No churn, refunds, or amendments: Contract runs full term. Early termination would reverse unearned DR and reduce RPO immediately.
